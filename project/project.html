Goal Oriented Academy

as if for now Goal Oriented Academy is a programming academy which has over 2700 members. Here children in Georgia learn about programming and different languages (for programming) like: Python, JavaScript, CSS, HTML.

But that's not the only thing we learn here. We make good friends who, instead of being dead weight, help us improve ourselves and bring out the best in us. We have hard competition and dedication to move up, and when you know enough, you can start sharing that knowledge with others. GOA helps us improve and prepare for the future here we get goals and skills to compleate them this is not just an academy its a big family when everyone cares for eachother.
